Who is the Course For?

  • For you, if you want to be skilled.

What Are The Benefits?

  • You will learn a popular skill in only 4 weeks.
  • You will get a mega confidence boost.
  • You will be able to make money in a short period after the course by getting a job or freelancing.

What is your return on investment (ROI)?

  • Very High
  • The course fee is only 40,000
  • After completion & practicing your skills for only 30 days, you will be good enough to be hired as a junior graphic designer for a minimum of 15-20,000 / month.

This course is not for?
