Accommodation

99% of our students come from outside of Mirpur and thus we know accomodation is a major issue for our students. Subject to availability we have accomodation we can offer you on a first come, first basis. This needs to be booked as soon as your seat for a course is confirmed.

Rooms are offered on a shared basis, monthly rent is 6,000 per person per month for month long courses. 2 weeks courses are charged at 3500 per person. We DO NOT OFFER FOOD.

If our accomodation is not to your liking, we would be more than happy to find you alternative local accomodation with hostels or 3/4/5 star hotels. For this you can call us or write to us.
